1. A Random Walk moves in randomly selected directions.
2. Random Walk is widely used in mathematics, physics, biology, and computer science.
3. It models real-world movements such as animal paths, molecule motion, and stock price simulations.
4. `random.choice()` randomly selects one direction from a list.
5. `setheading()` changes the Turtle's direction instantly.
6. `pensize()` changes the thickness of the drawing line.
7. `speed()` controls how fast the Turtle animation runs.
8. Random colors make the drawing more visually appealing.
9. Loops repeatedly move the Turtle to create a continuous random path.
10. Random Walk is a simple but powerful simulation technique.
